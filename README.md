# Public-Health-Research-Project
This project focuses on how health informatics contributes to emerging areas: Evaluating the Effectiveness and Barriers of m-health-based interventions promoting lifestyle changes among Adults with prediabetes

### Objectives:
This project aimed to evaluate the use of m-health based intervention in promoting lifestyle changes in Adult prediabetes. As one of the emrging areas or field in health informatics that lies at the intersection of
health and technology to improve patient outcomes.

Methodology:

This research project was done by reviewing papers related to the study and topic as well as understanding the concept behind mobile based technology to help promote life style changes.
The project included what prediabtes is, why it matters, what is m-health and what is its impact on public health. And some related research work related to this topic was highlighted as well as the limitations, barriers,
of this topic.


## Result:
The project highlighted the world wide population of people with dibates which are over 400 million and out of this number 20% of the population in US come down with prediabetes which is a huge number and the health care cost or burden for 
for this is over 327 billion anually for the USA. This in itself is a major factor that requires effective prevention strategy to improve Diabetes Prevention Program. Hence the need for m-based health as 4 out of 5 americans have cell
phones which can be used to manage such situations. m-based health are mobile technologies used for the purpose of improving healthcare , delive health care remotely and example of such are: werable devices, digital platforms,
mobile phones. Advantage of this is that it is accessible and can be used anywhere. Additionally the imapct of this on public healthis cost reduction, data monitoring, disease prevention. Some of the challenges with this are:
digital divide, privacy and security concerns and digital literacy especially amongst older adults.
